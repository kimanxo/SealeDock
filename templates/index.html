Features Overview

Full Ownership & Control

Gain complete sovereignty over your data. As the sole owner, you have the unrestricted ability to encrypt, decrypt, share, and manage your files according to your needs.

Flexible Encryption Options

Choose from industry-standard encryption algorithms like AES and RSA. Secure your files with a personal password or utilize an auto-generated, robust key for maximum security. Files are stored temporarily, automatically deleting after a configurable period (1-6 hours, default 6 hours) for enhanced privacy.

Secure Decryption & Key Storage

Access and decrypt your files whenever you need them. Your unique decryption keys are securely stored within your account, encrypted at the database level, ensuring only the system (Django backend) can access them for your operations, preventing unauthorized access or leaks.

Versatile Download Choices

Download your decrypted files in their original format or choose to package them as a password-protected ZIP archive for an extra layer of security during transfer or storage.

One-Time Sharing Keys

Securely share files with others using temporary, single-use decryption keys.

Secure File Previews

Generate time-limited preview links for others to view files without downloading. You can monitor link status and manually reactivate them from your dashboard.

Robust Account Security
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Enable MFA for an additional layer of security on your account.
  • Activity Logs: Track access to your shared files, including when and potentially by whom (based on key usage).
  • Profile Management: Easily update your profile picture, password, and other settings.
  • Key Security: Your decryption keys remain hidden, even from you. They are securely stored and encrypted in the database, accessible only by the application logic.
Roles & Permissions

Manage collaboration with defined roles:

  • Owner: Has complete control over group files, encryption, and member management, including assigning Admins.
  • Admin: Can encrypt files, manage preview links, and invite new users to the group.
  • Member: Can decrypt and download group files but cannot encrypt new ones.
Shared Group Encryption Key

Each group utilizes a single, shared encryption key. Files encrypted by Admins using this key can be decrypted by any member of the group, simplifying secure collaboration.

Detailed Audit Logs

Maintain comprehensive audit trails for group activities. Track exactly who decrypted which file and when. Logs for file creators include detailed metadata like IP address, timestamp, file details, and other available information for thorough accountability.
