Privacy Policy

At SealeDock, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that you have full control over your encrypted files.

1. Data Ownership

All encrypted files you upload are owned by you. You retain full control over encryption, decryption, sharing, and deletion. No one—not even our team—can access your decryption keys or view your data.

2. Encryption and Security

  • Files are encrypted using industry-standard algorithms such as AES or RSA.
  • You may provide your own password or let the system generate one securely.
  • Decryption keys are stored securely and encrypted within our system—accessible only through the platform, not by any human.
  • Files are temporarily stored and auto-deleted after 1 to 6 hours (default: 6 hours).

3. File Sharing

Sharing is optional and always under your control. You can generate a one-time key to allow another user to decrypt a file. Once used, that key is permanently destroyed.

4. Temporary File Previews

You may generate preview links that expire after a limited time. In case of brute-force attempts, preview links are disabled automatically and can only be manually regenerated by the creator.

5. Group Features & Activity Logs

In group mode, access is permission-based. Group owners and admins define what members can do. All activity—including file decryption and access—is logged with timestamps and IP metadata for transparency and auditing.

6. Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information—only what's necessary to manage your account (e.g., email, username). None of your content is used for analytics, ads, or third-party sharing.

7. MFA and Security Options

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is available to add an extra layer of protection to your account and actions such as sharing files.

8. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your account and all related data at any time. Once removed, this action is irreversible.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Major changes will always be communicated to you directly through your dashboard or email.
